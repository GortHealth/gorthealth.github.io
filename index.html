About Kinly

Bring your family back to the living room with Kinly

Family

Kinly is a web based app that connects families to their elderly loved ones without having to learn new technologies. Our mission is reduce social isolation among seniors by making family engagement easier than ever. Kinly allows families to upload text, photo & video content onto a family channel accessible from the senior’s television.

The Elderly

At Kinly we understand it can be difficult for your elderly loved one to learn to use new devices and tech. That’s why we’ve made social engagement accessible in a format familiar to almost every generation: the television. Staying connected is as easy as using a remote. Seniors can tune into the family channel any time to get back to being engaged in family life.

Healthcare Providers

We made Kinly plug & play. Since it’s compatible for every television and remote, all you have to do is follow the pre-installed instructions & it’s good to go! Everyone has their own way of doing things. That’s why we give family members complete control over what content to share and how it appears on your elderly loved one’s television.

History

The Kinly Story

  •

    January 2016

    Bitmaker

    The Kinly dev team enrolls at Bitmaker; a tech accelerator located in the heart of Toronto. Coming from diverse backgrounds in graphic design, engineering, business, and more, the team decides to sign up for a hackathon over sushi.

  • February 2016

    Hacking Health Hamilton

    The Kinly team attends Hacking Health Hamilton. After picking up a healthcare professional (and an intense 36 hours), Kinly performs a live demonstration with a working prototype. Kinly wins top prize and best business.

  • April 2016

    Preacceleration and Validation

    As part of the prize for winning Hacking Health Hamilton, Kinly receives mentorship and support services from Cossette Health and Innovation Factory. The Kinly team performs market intelligence research and product validations.

  • Apps4Health

    Kinly members Frances and Ariel present the product and team as part of an expert panel on entrepreneurship in healthcare technologies at the Apps4Health 2016 conference.

  • Today

    Technical Development

    The Kinly team is working on building Kinly into a fully functional product that provides an exceptional user experience for both the family and their elderly loved ones.
